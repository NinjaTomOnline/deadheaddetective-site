DeadHead Detective Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

DeadHead Detective is a utility app for reviewing trips, route segments, paid miles, deadhead miles, and profitability. This policy is written for the current public launch scope described on this site.

Information the app may store

Based on the current app features available today, DeadHead Detective may store information you choose to create or allow the app to derive, including:

  • trip sessions, route segments, timestamps, mileage totals, and platform labels
  • session notes, profitability settings, notification preferences, and retention settings
  • local runtime details such as recent notification history or diagnostics used to support in-app troubleshooting
  • export files you intentionally generate through the app’s export flow

How permissions are used

  • Location: used to detect trips, map routes, and distinguish paid travel from deadhead travel.
  • Motion & Activity: used to improve trip start and stop detection and reduce unnecessary tracking noise.
  • Notifications: used for trip-start alerts, deadhead warnings, and session summaries when those features are enabled.

Storage And Sharing

How your data is handled

The current launch scope is built as a personal utility app, not a public profile, team dashboard, or ad-driven social service.

On-device focus

The app is designed around local trip history, route review, notifications, and settings stored for your own use on your device.

No public sharing feature in launch scope

No public account directory, social feed, or in-app browsing of other drivers’ trip history is part of the current launch promise.

Exports are user-directed

If you choose to export data, the resulting files are created because you requested them and are then handled by the iOS share flow or any destination you choose.

Retention And Deletion

Controls available in the current app

The current settings surface includes explicit data-retention and deletion controls, which this page should stay aligned with as the app evolves.

Retention

The app includes retention choices inside Settings so you can decide how long app data should remain available locally for your own use.

Delete All Data

The app includes a destructive delete-all-data flow intended to remove saved trips, route segments, exported earnings data, and saved settings from the device where the action is taken.
