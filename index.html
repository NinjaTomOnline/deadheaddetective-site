DeadHead Detective

Catch the unpaid miles before they eat your shift.

DeadHead Detective is a trip and profitability tracker for gig driving. The current app tracks active trips, measures paid miles versus deadhead miles, and gives you Dashboard, History, Insights, and privacy controls in one focused iPhone and iPad experience.

Track trips as they happen

DeadHead Detective uses location and motion signals to arm auto-tracking, keep an eye on active trips, and show live status while a drive is underway.

Separate paid miles from deadhead miles

The app is built around the core question gig drivers ask every week: how much of the route actually paid, and how much of it was unpaid repositioning.

Review every session

History and trip detail views keep route maps, session notes, earnings context, and deadhead cost in one place.

See patterns by platform

Dashboard and Insights highlight trends, worth-it scoring, platform mix, and recurring route patterns for services like DoorDash, Uber Eats, Lyft, Instacart, and personal miles.

Keep control of your data

Settings include notification controls, export tools, retention choices, and a destructive delete-all-data flow for local cleanup when you need it.

Inside The App

A focused utility for route truth, not hype

The current build centers on the screens that are already in the app: Dashboard, Active Trip, History, Insights, and Settings.

Dashboard

Review worth-it scoring, mileage trends, recent trips, and platform stats without needing a spreadsheet first.

Active Trip

See live trip state, route updates, deadhead warnings, and quick controls to start, pause, resume, or end a trip.

History + Trip Detail

Open prior sessions to inspect route maps, segment timelines, notes, and the actual cost of repositioning.

Insights + Settings

Compare platform performance, review deadhead trends, tune thresholds and notifications, and manage export or deletion controls from one place.

Launch Scope

What this public site promises

Everything below is grounded in the app surfaces and release notes that are already part of the current launch scope.

Included in the current site promise

  • iPhone and iPad app experience focused on gig-driving trip tracking
  • Auto-tracking, live trip state, trip history, and deadhead-aware session review
  • Dashboard and Insights views for trends, worth-it score, and platform comparisons
  • Trip-start, deadhead, and summary notifications when permissions and settings allow them
  • Settings flows for export, retention, privacy review, diagnostics, and delete-all-data controls

Not part of the public launch promise

  • Guaranteed Apple Watch or widget experience for launch
  • Shared accounts, team features, or public social profiles
  • Advanced route recommendation or machine-learning coaching beyond the current in-app insights
